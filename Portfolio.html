THE MVP

2024

>>> Introduction

A music video got released around the same time school started called “The MVP” by Fimiguerreo. I think I watched the music video 10 times that night and I immediately said this will be my portfolio look for this semester.(strongly recommend to watch)

>>> Nostalgia

I connected to this aesthetic quickly because it reminded me of when I was growing up owning a Black Berry, watching Kick Buttowski, playing video games like GTA and Just Dance. But overall I just loved the look of the music video.

>>> Moodboard & Recreations

I made a moodboard on pinterest and started making my own designs. I felt like this was a good idea to understand and better the look for myself. With each one I felt like I was making the designs better and able to add more elements.

>>> Landing pages


Then I thought of making landing pages. I made three different ones playing around how I would structure it. I went and asked Jan what he thought about the design cause I felt like it was gonna be a very bold and different design. He liked the style but he told me I have a problem with the hierarchy of my design. Other things would catch his eye before important things. He told me to work on that. I made more iterations and asked for feedback on which one looked the best to continue.

<

>>> Work Pages

I wanted to try a more creative look for the information on the project page but teacher's feedback told me that the less creative look would be better for the information which is more important. I decided to mix both looks to have the best of both worlds.

>>> Signature Logo

Recently Detroit Pistons Cade Cunningham has come out with his new logo which has two C’s shaped into a star. As I was looking at the logo I saw an A. so then I thought I could try using my initial to make a star. I made a few iterations before landing one. Which I am very happy with I feel like this logo represents me as a person and its nice to have my initials corporated into a star
